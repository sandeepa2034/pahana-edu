    Frequently Asked Questions

    To browse and purchase books:

    1. Visit our Shop page to view all available books
    2. Use filters to search by category, price range, or keywords
    3. Click "Add to Cart" for books you want to purchase
    4. Review your cart and proceed to checkout
    5. No account creation required for purchases!

    No! You can browse and purchase books as a guest without creating an account.

    However, creating an account allows you to:

    • Save your favorite books
    • Track your order history
    • Receive personalized recommendations
    • Get updates on new arrivals

    We offer books in various categories including:

    • Novels: Fiction stories, literature classics
    • History: Historical events, biographies, culture
    • Scifi: Science fiction, futuristic stories
    • Educational: Academic texts, learning materials
    • Children's: Kids' books, picture books, stories

    You can find books using several methods:

    • Search Bar: Enter book title, author name, or keywords
    • Category Filter: Browse books by specific categories
    • Price Range: Filter books within your budget
    • Sort Options: Sort by title, author, or price

    We accept the following payment methods for your convenience:

    • Cash on Delivery (COD)
    • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex)
